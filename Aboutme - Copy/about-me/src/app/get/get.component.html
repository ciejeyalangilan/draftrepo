Get to know me

To be a Surgeon

In this world, becoming a doctor is a prestigious career. Doctors give hope to people suffering from severe and chronic disease, which is why I want to be a good doctor. I wish to help the impoverished residents who are in need of medicine. I aspire to assist individuals that do not have access to medical care and I really want to give them hope.

Photography

I've loved taking photography since I was a child because a picture is worth a thousand words. Because photography captures a scene that lasts forever, it keeps memories alive and affluent. Each day seems to be something worth bearing in mind, whether it is a past event or a present one.

A Sunset

I enjoy watching stunning sunsets and occasionally capture them. I think I've never felt like I could express that feeling accurately. When I watch a beautiful sunset, I feel a sense of serenity and contentment in my heart. It is something I, for one, really need in my life.

Traveling

Traveling, since all great people actually believe, is possibly the only way to discover the world and its cultures. I enjoy traveling because it provides me with a sense of discovery and freedom.

Drinking Coffee

I could talk to someone for hours over coffee, whether it's merely chit-chat or deep and serious talks. Coffee's warmth and liveliness serve as the spark for any and all interactions. Not only do I enjoy the aroma of coffee, but I really love learning about the many cultures of coffee.
